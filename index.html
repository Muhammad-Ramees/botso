01 / About Us

Your Simple Startup Launchpad

Stuck with a brilliant idea but unsure how to turn it real? Relax, Botso's here! We're your startup launchpad, helping turn awesome ideas into amazing apps, websites, or anything techy you need.

FAQs :

+
What's an MVP and how can it help my startup?

MVP, or Minimum Viable Product, is a basic version of your idea with just enough features to test its market potential and gather user feedback. It helps you validate your concept, save resources, and iterate based on real-world data before investing heavily in full-fledged development.


+
Can Botso help me raise funding for my startup?

While we don't directly connect you with investors, we can certainly prepare your startup for success in attracting funding. We build high-quality, data-driven MVPs that demonstrate market traction and user validation, crucial points for potential investors. We also offer guidance on crafting compelling pitches and fundraising strategies.


+
I don't have any technical experience. Can I still work with Botso?

Absolutely! Our experienced team is here to guide you through every step of the process, from initial concept to launch. We translate complex technical aspects into clear and understandable language, ensuring you're involved and informed throughout the journey. We handle the development complexities, allowing you to focus on your vision and business strategy.
